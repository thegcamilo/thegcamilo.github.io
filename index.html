Research Interests

I am interested in research areas where machines and humans intersect, such as computational social science and applications of machine learning. My main goal is to understand people's perception of AI and how AI will morally, ethically and legally affect our society. I intend to work closely with social scientists and policymakers in order to better understand the consequences of the insertion of AI and robots in our society and how we can take advantage of these technologies while mitigating the possible negative effects.
