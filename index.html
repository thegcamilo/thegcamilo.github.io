Gabriel Lima

I am a computer science undergraduate student and a research intern in the Data Science Group at IBS advised by Meeyoung Cha. You can check my CV here.

I am interested in understanding people's perception of artificial intelligence (AI) and how AI will morally and legally affect our society. I am currently working towards understanding how the general public assigns responsibility for the actions of AI and robots.
